It's never too late to start exercising, and in order for it to be a pleasant pastime with a gradual load, you should first prepare yourself.

    What to avoid ˎˊ˗

      ✿ Overtraining:

      Sudden start with too high weights or frequent exercise can cause serious damage to your body.

      ✿ Heavy workouts:

      Wear-out workouts with a marked deterioration in well-being (acute pain, dizziness, severe shortness of breath).

      ✿ Missing warmups:

      Missing warmups and hitches are some of the most common causes of injury.

      ✿ Wrong posture and technique:

      Incorrect posture and too fast pace without mastering the technique.
