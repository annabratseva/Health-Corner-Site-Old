Stuffed peppers in the oven

Total cooking time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 8 pcs Bell Peppers
  • 200 ml Rice
  • 1 cup Onion
  • 1 pc Carrot (one large or two small)
  • 100 g Green Beans
  • 100 g Canned Corn
  • 50 g Hard Cheese
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil (for frying)
  • Ground Black Pepper — to taste
  • Salt — to taste

Step 1: Prepare the ingredients. Wash and dry the vegetables thoroughly.

Step 2: Chop the onion finely.

Step 3: Grate the carrot or chop it into small pieces.

Step 4: Heat a frying pan over low heat, pour vegetable oil on it. Add the onion and carrot. Fry them, stirring, for 5 minutes, until soft.

Step 5: Rinse the rice in several waters, drain the remaining water well. Add the rice to the pan along with the vegetables. Mix the products. Fry the rice for a couple of minutes, stirring.

Step 6: Pour 2/3 of the rice volume of boiling water into the pan. Add salt and pepper. You can also add your favorite spices.

Step 7: Cover the pan with a lid and leave the rice to cook over low heat.

Step 8: When all the liquid is absorbed, add the beans to the rice. Stir and simmer for another couple of minutes.

Step 9: Then turn off the heat and add the corn. Mix it up. The filling is ready. The rice turns out to be semi-cooked, as it should be, it will finally cook in the oven.

Step 10: Cut off the tails of the peppers along with part of the pulp, but do not throw them away. They will act as caps. Remove the middle along with the seeds. The pepper in this recipe is not pre-boiled.

Step 11: Stuff the prepared pepper with the filling. Place them standing in a baking dish. Only 6 pieces fit into mine, although the filling was enough for 8 medium peppers.

Step 12: Cover the peppers with lids so that the filling does not dry out during baking. Place the pepper pan in the oven, preheated to 180 °C. Bake the peppers for 30 minutes. Focus on your oven.

Step 13: Five minutes before the end, you can sprinkle the peppers with grated cheese on top. If you are preparing a lean meal or following a vegetarian diet, then eliminate this step.

Step 14: Remove the finished peppers from the oven, cool slightly and serve. Enjoy your meal!
