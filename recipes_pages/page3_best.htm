Fruit yogurt

Total cooking time: 10 min

Ingredients:

  • 200 g Yogurt (Classic, without additives and sugar)
  • 110 g Bananas
  • 70 g Mandarins
  • 50 g Pomegranates

Step 1: Peel the banana and cut into small circles. Transfer it to the bowl of a blender.

Step 2: Peel the tangerine and check for pits. It is better to get rid of them, especially if you serve yogurt to children. Cut the tangerine into slices and put it in a blender.

Step 3: Wash the pomegranate under running water and peel it. Separate the pomegranate seeds and add them to the blender bowl.

Step 4: Take a classic yogurt without additives and pour it into the bowl of a blender.

Step 5: Blend all ingredients until smooth, starting in pulse mode, then increasing speed. Pour the finished yogurt into cups, garnish with fruit if desired, and serve. Enjoy!
