Salad with tomato,cucumber and egg

Total cooking time: 10 min

Ingredients:

  • Cucumber — 1 pc
  • Cherry tomatoes (or 2 large) — 12 pcs
  • Eggs — 2 pcs
  • Lettuce leaves — 5 pcs
  • Sour cream — 3 tbsp
  • Salt — to taste
  • Herbs — to taste
  • Ground black pepper — to taste

Step 1: Use any salad greens (iceberg, arugula, romano, etc.); I used regular leaf lettuce. Cherry tomatoes can replace large ones. Boil the eggs in advance and let them cool. Use herbs to taste (I used parsley). Wash and dry all vegetables.

Step 2: Wash the lettuce leaves and dry them well with a paper towel. This is important to do, as excess moisture will spoil the salad.

Step 3: Slice the cucumber into rings. Taste it to ensure it's not bitter. If the skin is smooth and undamaged, no need to peel.

Step 4: Cut the tomatoes into small pieces. Cherry is enough to cut in half, the larger ones into slices.

Step 5: To avoid cracking, boil eggs in cold water over low heat for 9 minutes, then cool in cold water for easier peeling. Cut into quarters or smaller, depending on size. Quail eggs make the salad look especially nice.

Step 6: Use a flat plate or salad bowl. Tear lettuce by hand (not with a knife) to prevent wilting from metal contact.

Step 7: Place the cucumber and tomato slices on the salad.

Step 8: Arrange the egg pieces on top. Also, tear the greens with your hands and spread them over the salad.

Step 9: Spoon sour cream between the egg pieces. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately, as fresh salads release juice and don’t keep long. Enjoy!
