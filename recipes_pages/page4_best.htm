Red fish in cream sauce in the oven

Total cooking time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 400 g Red fish fillet
  • 15 g Dill (2–3 sprigs)
  • 7 g Thyme (2 sprigs)
  • 1 tablespoon Sunflower oil
  • Ground black pepper — to taste
  • Salt — to taste

    • For the sauce:

  • 200 ml Cream (10%)
  • 100 g Processed cheese
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 2 tbsp  Olive oil

Step 1: Prepare the ingredients. You can take any red fish, as long as it's a fillet. Wash and dry the fish and herbs well.

Step 2: Season the red fish fillet with salt and pepper, place in a baking dish, top with thyme, and bake at 200°C for 20 minutes. Let rest in the oven for 15 more minutes.

Step 3: Prepare the sauce ingredients: use any cream (higher fat gives richer taste), plain processed cheese, and any vegetable oil if olive oil isn’t available.

Step 4: Peel and finely chop the garlic.

Step 5: Heat olive oil in a pan and briefly sauté chopped garlic until fragrant, but don't let it burn.

Step 6: Pour in the cream while stirring and simmer on low heat for about 10 minutes until fully combined with the oil.

Step 7: Add the melted cheese to the pan and mix until smooth. You can cut it into pieces, but it will disperse quickly from exposure to temperature.

Step 8: Finely chop the washed and dried dill.

Step 9: Remove the fish from the oven and transfer to a plate. Sprinkle the dill on top and pour the cream sauce over it. Enjoy your meal!
